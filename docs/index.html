Frequently Asked Questions

Is this extension free?

Yes, Patreon Exporter is completely free and open source. No hidden fees, no premium features, no subscriptions.

Does this work with private Patreon posts?

Yes, the extension works with any Patreon post you have access to, including subscriber-only content. It uses your existing login session.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers, and we don't track your usage.

Can I customize the PDF output?

Yes, you can choose image quality levels and whether to include comments. PDFs are exported in A4 format with optimized pagination.
